Settings:








































Hotkeys

  • W/↑ - Move Up
  • S/↓ - Move Down
  • A/← - Move Left
  • D/→ - Move Right
  • Shift - Half Movement Speed
  • Z/J - Use First Ability
  • X/K - Use Second Ability
  • C/L - Use Third Ability
  • H - Hide Herocard
  • M - Hide Minimap
  • , - Hide Overlay

Cheats:

  • T - Teleport Forward
  • E - Teleport Backward
  • R - Teleport 10 Areas Forward
  • V - God Mode
  • B - Unlimited Energy
  • N - No Collision

Test Mode:

  • [ - Create SafePoint
  • ] - Clear SafePoint
  • P - Change Timer Clear
  • O - Reset Timer
  • END - Reset Your Run
Replay Settings:
















Replay Controls

  • Space - Play/Pause
  • ← - Rewind 5 seconds
  • → - Fast forward 5 seconds
  • ↑ - Increase speed
  • ↓ - Decrease speed
  • R - Reset to start
  • F - Toggle fullscreen
  • C - Cycle camera views
  • M - Toggle minimap
  • H - Toggle hitboxes

Camera Controls:

  • WASD - Move camera
  • Q/E - Zoom in/out
  • Mouse Drag - Rotate view
  • Mouse Wheel - Adjust zoom
